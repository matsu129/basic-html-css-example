Museums list in Canada

About

This website introduces the top 10 ranked museums in Canada.
From art galleries to history and science museums, you can discover the best places to learn about Canadian culture, history, and creativity. Explore these popular museums and find your next destination in Canada!

  1. Royal Ontario Museum


    Royal Ontario Museum website

    Located in Toronto, this is the largest museum of world cultures and natural history in Canada.

  2. Canadian Museum of History


    Canadian Museum of History website

    One of the most visited museums in Canada, located in Gatineau, Quebec, exploring the nation's history and heritage.

  3. Montreal Museum of Fine Arts


    Montreal Museum of Fine Arts website

    A major art museum in Montreal with a vast collection of artworks and exhibitions.

  4. Royal BC Museum


    Royal BC Museum website

    Located in Victoria, BC, this museum features natural and human history exhibits of the region.

  5. Art Gallery of Ontario


    Art Gallery of Ontario website

    One of North America's largest art museums, with over 90,000 works in Toronto.

  6. Canadian Museum for Human Rights


    Canadian Museum for Human Rights website

    Located in Winnipeg, this museum is dedicated to the evolution, celebration, and future of human rights.

  7. Royal Tyrrell Museum


    Royal Tyrrell Museum website

    Famous for its dinosaur fossils and paleontology exhibits, located in Drumheller, Alberta.

  8. UBC Museum of Anthropology


    UBC Museum of Anthropology website

    Located in Vancouver, this museum is renowned for its First Nations art and artifacts.

  9. Manitoba Museum


    Manitoba Museum website

    The largest museum in Winnipeg, featuring natural and human history exhibits.

  10. Canadian War Museum


    Canadian War Museum website

    Located in Ottawa, this museum focuses on Canada's military history and heritage.
